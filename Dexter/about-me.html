Origin Story

Who I am

Computer Science student at Strathmore University, navigating the intersection of software engineering and computational biology. I believe the best code solves real problems for real people — whether that's connecting food donors to charities in Nairobi or automating IT workflows at a government agency.

What drives me

The gap between who you are and who the role needs you to be — that's where growth lives. From leading engineering sprints at Jackal Tech to building community platforms like FoodShare, I've learned that shipping beats perfection, and building for your own community carries a different kind of weight.

Where I'm headed

Aspiring bioinformatician with a full-stack foundation. I'm drawn to genomics pipelines, RAG-based AI systems, and open-source hardware like RISC-V. The classroom gives you a foundation — the work gives you everything else.

Beyond the screen

IEEE Strathmore Publicity Lead, active in the Cybersecurity Students Club, and always curious about data science communities. When I'm not coding, you'll find me at workshops on ethical hacking, RISC-V chip design, or the latest Strathmore research expo.
